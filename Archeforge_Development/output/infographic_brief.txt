3.  STEP-BY-STEP WORKFLOW: BUILD YOUR AI SUBJECT LINE GENERATOR

    (Each step will be a distinct, numbered section with concise text.)

    STEP 1: GET STARTED WITH ZAPIER
    *   Text: Sign up or log in to Zapier.com. Create a new "Zap" – this is your automation workflow.
    *   Tool Callout: Zapier (logo)

    STEP 2: SET YOUR TRIGGER (INPUT)
    *   Text: Tell Zapier when to start your automation.
    *   a. App: Select Dropbox.
    *   b. Event: Choose "New File in Folder."
    *   c. Connect: Link your Dropbox account & pick your input folder (e.g., "Drop Zone Emails").
    *   d. Test: Place a sample text file in the folder to confirm detection.
    *   Tool Callout: Dropbox (logo)

    STEP 3: ADD AI ACTION (PROCESSING)
    *   Text: Let AI process your input and generate content.
    *   a. App: Select ChatGPT (or OpenAI).
    *   b. Event: Choose "Conversation."
    *   c. Connect: Link your OpenAI account & select model (e.g., "gpt-4").
    *   d. Prompt: Craft your prompt (e.g., "Generate five email subject lines for this email:").
    *   e. Input: Pass "File Text" from your Dropbox trigger into the prompt.
    *   f. Test: Verify ChatGPT generates relevant subject lines.
    *   Tool Callout: ChatGPT / OpenAI (logo)

    STEP 4: SAVE YOUR RESULTS (OUTPUT)
    *   Text: Store the AI-generated content in your preferred location.
    *   a. App: Select Dropbox.
    *   b. Event: Choose "Create Text File."
    *   c. Connect: Link Dropbox & choose your output folder (e.g., "Subject Lines").
    *   d. File Name: Define using original file name + "_AutoSubject.txt" for clarity.
    *   e. File Content: Use the "Assistant Response Message" from the ChatGPT step.
    *   f. Test: Confirm a new file appears in your output folder with the subject lines.
    *   Tool Callout: Dropbox (logo)

    STEP 5: ACTIVATE & TEST
    *   Text: Publish your Zap to activate the automation. Drop a new email into your input folder and watch the subject lines appear automatically in your output folder. Your AI assistant is now live 24/7!

4.  TOOL & SERVICE CALLOUTS (Bottom of infographic or as a sidebar)
    *   Zapier: Connect & Automate
